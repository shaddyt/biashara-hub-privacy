Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

Welcome to Biashara Hub ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Biashara Hub mobile application ("App").

Summary: We only collect the information necessary to provide our inventory and sales management services. We never sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Biashara Hub, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Shop name, password, location, and contact information (customer care name)
  • Business Data: Product names, categories, quantities, buying prices, and selling prices
  • Sales Records: Transaction details, quantities sold, customer names (optional), and sale dates
  • Subscription Information: Payment details for premium features (if applicable)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (features used, frequency of use)
  • Crash reports and error logs for app improvement
  • Network status information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our inventory and sales management services
  • Authenticate your account and ensure data security
  • Generate reports and summaries for your business
  • Send important notifications about your account or the App
  • Improve our App features and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscription payments (if applicable)

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols
  • Password Protection: Your passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption and never stored in plain text
  • Secure Servers: Your data is stored on secure cloud servers with restricted access
  • Data Isolation: Each shop's data is isolated and cannot be accessed by other users
  • Regular Backups: We maintain regular backups to prevent data loss

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our App (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (your data would remain protected under this policy)
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct Your Data: Update or correct inaccurate information through the App settings
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export Your Data: Download your business data (products, sales records)
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of non-essential data collection at any time

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: We delete your data within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Backup Data: Backup copies are purged within 90 days of account deletion

7. Children's Privacy

Biashara Hub is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Our App may use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure database hosting and authentication
  • Expo: For app development and updates
  • Payment Providers: For processing subscription payments (if applicable)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with an updated date
  • Sending a notification through the App
  • Emailing you if the changes are significant

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
